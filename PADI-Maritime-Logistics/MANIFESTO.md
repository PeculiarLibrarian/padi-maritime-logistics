### 1. The Core Philosophy
The Sovereign Bureau rejects the ambiguity of unstructured data. In the context of the **PADI Maritime Standard**, we assert that integrity is not a preference—it is a deterministic requirement.

- **Knowledge Representation:** OWL 2 
- **Integrity Enforcement:** SHACL (Shapes Constraint Language)

- **Regression Suite:** Passmark/Unittest

### 3. The Nairobi-01-Node Verification
